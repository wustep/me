# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Curious readers, designers, builders, and collaborators exploring Stephen Wu's work and ideas. On the Lenses surface, they are browsing a visual deck of mental models, opening whichever concept catches their attention, and using it to reconsider a familiar problem from a new angle.

## Product Purpose

wustep.me is Stephen's personal home on the web: a place to communicate how he thinks, what he makes, and what he finds useful. Lenses turns a growing body of mental models into an inviting spatial collection. Success means visitors feel rewarded for wandering, understand that no single model is complete, and leave with one useful perspective they can apply.

## Brand Personality

Curious, incisive, and playful. The voice should feel intellectually serious without becoming academic, visually confident without becoming precious, and generous enough to make complex ideas approachable.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, uniform template card grids, sterile productivity-app minimalism, and ornamental editorial styling that makes the ideas feel more rarefied than useful. Avoid gamified novelty, visual noise that competes with reading, and interaction flourishes that obscure what is clickable or where focus is.

## Design Principles

- Make exploration feel purposeful: wandering should continually reveal structure, relationships, and a clear next move.
- Give every idea a memorable visual handle without reducing it to decoration.
- Practice the content's own values: use constraints deliberately, keep interfaces legible, and let utility earn attention.
- Reward curiosity quickly while preserving depth for readers who choose to linger.
- Keep the system coherent, but let individual lenses retain distinct color and character.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Preserve complete keyboard navigation, visible focus, semantic controls, sufficient text contrast, touch-friendly targets, dark-mode parity, and a motion-reduced experience that communicates the same information without animation.
